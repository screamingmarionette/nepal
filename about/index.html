Band Bio

Screaming Marionette is a modern metal band, currently based in Australia, consisting of musicians of Nepalese origin, who aim to carry heavy metal music to global level. The band specializes in blending heavy modern metal sound with elements of atmospheres, melody, intensity and cinematic sound design in order to explore new horizons of heavy music while carrying massive energetic live shows.


Channeling the spirit of contemporary metal while carrying the weight of their own cultural perspective, Screaming Marionette conjures primal yet expansive sounds that speak to the universal human experience of identity, alienation, strife, and victory. The music of the band reflects their own personal struggle as a group of emerging artists building a voice for themselves in the farthest corners of the land.


Since the band’s inception, Screaming Marionette have been making waves in the Australian heavy music scene by touring regional areas and cities across the country and sharing the stage with both local and international acts. These shows are always heavy on the intensity, heavy on the musicianship and heavy on atmosphere.
